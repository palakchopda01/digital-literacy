# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students  

## ✅ General Safety Tips  
- 🔐 **Use strong, unique passwords** for all accounts (avoid reusing passwords).
- 🔑 **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on email, social media, and banking apps.
- 📱 **Lock your devices** with a PIN, password, or fingerprint.
- 🌐 **Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities** like banking or logging into important accounts.
- 🧠 **Think before clicking links** in emails, messages, or social media—especially from unknown sources.
- 📥 **Download apps only from official stores** (Google Play Store or Apple App Store).
- 👀 **Review app permissions regularly** and remove unused apps.
- 💬 **Be cautious about what you share online** (location, phone number, personal details).

## 💰 UPI & Financial Safety Tips
- 🚫 **Never share your UPI PIN or OTP** with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.
- ✔️ **You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money**.
- ⚠️ **Always verify payment requests (“collect requests”)** before approving them.
- 📞 **Do not trust unknown callers asking for payment or account details**.

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime in India
- 🌐 Report online fraud at: **https://cybercrime.gov.in**
- 📞 Cyber Crime Helpline: **1930**

## 👍 Quick Reminder
Stay alert, stay informed, and act fast—most online frauds can be prevented with awareness.
