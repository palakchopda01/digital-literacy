✅ Do’s
Think before you post — consider how your content may be perceived by others.<br>
Maintain privacy settings to control who can view your personal information.  
Be respectful and professional in your interactions and comments.  
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.  
Use social media for positive engagement such as networking, learning, and collaboration.  
❌ Don’ts  
Don’t share sensitive personal information (e.g., address, phone number, passwords). 
Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.  
Don’t engage in online arguments or harassment.  
Don’t overshare details about your personal life that could affect your reputation.  
Don’t ignore your digital footprint — remember that posts can have long-term consequences.
